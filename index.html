Case studies: Mapping urban data

Walls of Air / Muros de Ar

Brazilian Pavilion Catalogue at Biennale di Architettura di Venezia 2018

This case study proposes a series of 10 maps with the theme "Walls of Air" developed and designed for the Brazilian Pavilion Catalogue at the Venice Biennale.

This project was created as a complementation of the main exhibition maps as a way of visualizing differents forms of "Walls" resulted from the Brazil's urbanization processes.

Each cartography includes several existing databases and researches articulated together with the curatorial team of the Brazilian Pavilion.
